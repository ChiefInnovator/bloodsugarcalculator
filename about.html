About DiabetesCalculator.ai

Our Mission

This eAG/A1C Conversion Calculator was created to provide individuals managing diabetes, healthcare professionals, and caregivers with a simple, reliable tool for converting between A1C percentages and estimated Average Glucose (eAG) values.

Our goal is to help users better understand their blood glucose levels over time, facilitating more informed discussions with healthcare providers and supporting effective diabetes management strategies. We also aim to provide clear educational resources on these important health metrics.

About the Tool

The eAG/A1C Conversion Calculator was developed to provide a straightforward way to convert between these two important diabetes metrics using the standard formulas recommended by organizations like the American Diabetes Association.

Key features include:

  • Simple interface for quick conversions.
  • Support for converting A1C to eAG (in both mg/dL and mmol/L).
  • Support for converting eAG (in both mg/dL and mmol/L) to A1C.
  • Client-side calculations for privacy (no data is sent to servers).
  • Educational resources explaining A1C, eAG, and factors affecting blood sugar.
  • Responsive design for use on various devices.
